Software and Systems Integration

Spring 2023

This course examines current and emerging trends, tactics, and techniques for designing efficient systems integration solutions. Topics include using business process models to capture integration requirements, applying integration patterns to design solutions, and leveraging service-oriented architecture to build integration solutions. Through lectures, case studies, projects, and assignments, students will develop a strong understanding of course concepts and gain hands-on experience with systems integration.

Prerequisites. SE411

Learning Outcomes  

This course has six learning outcomes:

  • CLO1: Identify key challenges, motivations, and approaches for systems integration projects.
  • CLO2: Apply organizational and managerial best practices to system integration initiatives.
  • CLO3: Employ important technologies, techniques, and architectures for systems integration such as service-oriented architecture, APIs, and middleware.
  • CLO4: Utilize business process models to define and analyze systems integration needs.
  • CLO5: Design workable solutions to integration problems using established integration patterns and frameworks.
  • CLO6: Develop and implement end-to-end system integration solutions using cutting-edge technologies and platforms.

Assessment  

This course involves occasional activities, labs and a semester-long project. The assignments and activities offers a chance for students to reinforce understanding of material from class.

Final course grades will be based on the following.

Activity Grade Details
Projects 40%
  • There will be several projects.
Presentations 5%
  • Each Student will present a 15 min. presentation. Evaluation criteria:
    • Content Quality: 40%
    • Delivery: 20%
    • Time Management: 10%
    • Presentation Design: 10%
    • Q and A: 20%
Midterm 15%
  • There will be 1 Midterm.
  • The Midterm will be on Week 8.
  • The Midterm will cover the materials finished by Week 7.
Final Exam 40% The Final Exam is a comprehensive exam covering all CLOs.
