About SEDI

About

The Software Engineering and Disruptive Innovation (SEDI) is aiming at bridging the gap between the technologies needed to overcome the economical-social-environmental challenges and the current existing technologies. SEDI performs fundamental and applied research for the specification, creation, evaluation and efficient operation of innovative, software-supported systems.

Vision

Leading organization in enhancing the efficiency, quality, and innovation to improve their global competitiveness.

Mission

Working with organizations in improving the process and content of their products/services by providing services that enhance their technical competence and improve their internal capabilities. To conduct applied and basic research on software engineering problems in order to enable technology gains in organizations.

SEDI Services

Consultation

SEDI center will provide consultation for its core expertise area such as secure software development, design optimization, process optimization, methodologies improvement, getting standardization, and working with international accreditation bodies.

Assessment

SEDI center will act as a professional accreditation body initially inside KSA and later globally for certifying software companies, the automation industry, multi-national organizations, local organizations, and academia for their processes and development. SEDI center will provide help to all clients, academic organizations, students, staff, and faculty in getting Professional certifications.

Training

The SEDI center will offer training related to its core areas leading to international certification in similar areas. Training will be a merge of both theoretical and practical expertise that will lead to fruitful training. The training will be offered in the area of Certified Secure Software Lifecycle Professional, CIW Web Development Professional, AWS Certified Developer, CISSP, CMMI, CISM, AWS, Microsoft, etc.
