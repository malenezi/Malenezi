About Prof. Alenezi

Professor Mamdouh Alenezi is a visionary leader and renowned expert in software engineering and artificial intelligence, currently serving as the Director of the AI Academy at Tahakom, the Saudi Technology and Security Comprehensive Control Company. With a strong foundation in software engineering, security, and AI, he has made a profound impact on the academic, scientific, and industry communities through his research and contributions. Professor Alenezi's work has been instrumental in advancing our understanding of how technology can be leveraged to improve lives, transform societies, and drive innovation. By applying data mining, machine learning, and AI techniques to address complex challenges, he has developed innovative solutions that have far-reaching implications for industries and communities worldwide. As a respected thought leader, Professor Alenezi has played a pivotal role in shaping the conversation around AI, software engineering, digital transformation, and cybersecurity. His research has explored the intersection and synergies between these fields, driving meaningful change and improvement in various sectors. Throughout his career, Professor Alenezi has demonstrated a commitment to excellence, earning recognition for his exceptional research and contributions to the field. He has secured funding from various organizations to conduct research on a wide range of AI and software engineering topics, and his expertise has been sought after by diverse projects in different sectors. As a dedicated educator and administrator, Professor Alenezi has held various leadership positions, including Chair of the Computer Science Department, Chief Information Technology Officer, and Dean of Quality Assurance and Development. In his current role, he continues to drive AI innovation and development initiatives that enhance the technological capabilities and promote excellence in the industry. Professor Alenezi's impact extends beyond the industry community, as his work has the potential to drive positive change and improvement in various aspects of society. His dedication to advancing knowledge and promoting innovation has earned him a reputation as a highly respected figure in the field of AI and software engineering.
